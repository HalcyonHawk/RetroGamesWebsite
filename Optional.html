OPTIONAL FEATURES

1.

Once the controller is set up, you can go to settings. No games yet. Here, you can add Wi-Fi by selecting your router and typing the password. A keyboard is needed. This can be used to transfer ROMs. However, I recommend doing the USB memory stick method for beginners or users on an open network.

2.

Create a folder called “retropie” on a blank USB. Then put this into the Raspberry Pi. This will create the needed folders. Put the USB back into your computer. Now, click and drag or copy and paste your ROMs into the correct folder for its console. After putting the USB into your raspberry Pi again, they will be on the SD card so there is no need to keep all the ROMs on the USB.
